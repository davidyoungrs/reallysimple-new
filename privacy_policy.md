# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 14, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to Contact Tree (Really Simple Apps). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy notice, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us.

## 2. Who we are and contact details
Contact Tree is the data controller responsible for deciding how and why your personal information is used. For any privacy-related questions or concerns, you may contact us at support@contacttree.link.

## 3. Information we collect
We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when interacting with our website, such as when creating an account, building your digital business card, or contacting us. This includes:
- **Personal Details:** Name, email address, phone number, bio, and profile photo.
- **Card Data:** Social media handles, custom links, and professional information you choose to display.
- **Usage Data:** We collect technical data indirectly, such as your IP address, browser type, and device type, primarily for analytics purposes to show you how your card is performing.

## 4. Legal basis for processing your information
We process your personal information based on:
- **Contractual Necessity:** To provide the services you signed up for.
- **Consent:** When you agree to specific processing, such as receiving updates.
- **Legitimate Interests:** To improve our platform and ensure security.

## 5. How we share your information
We do not sell your personal information. We only share data when necessary:
- **Public Profiles:** Information you add to your digital business card is intended to be shared publicly.
- **Service Providers:** We may use third-party tools (like Clerk for authentication and Supabase for database) to operate our service.
- **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose information if required by law.

## 6. Cookies and tracking technologies
We use cookies to maintain your session and understand how our site is being used. You can opt out of non-essential cookies through your browser settings.

## 7. Your data protection rights
Depending on your location (e.g., UK, EEA), you have rights under GDPR and other privacy laws, including:
- The right to access your data.
- The right to correct inaccurate data.
- The right to request deletion of your data.
- The right to data portability.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@contacttree.link.

## 8. US State Privacy Rights
Residents of California (CCPA) and Nevada have specific rights regarding access to and deletion of their personal information. We do not "sell" data to third parties.
